Our Story

During the study of children’s non-profits worldwide, it was observed that while children are provided for through the gracious acts of organizations and donors worldwide, teenagers aging out of their sponsorship programs are left vulnerable. It is because of this growing concern that Bridging Futures was founded. To address the gap years and prevent the progress in education from halting and giving these teenagers the opportunity to continue learning and growing

Our Approach

We work with vetted non-profits in the area of child development and motivated donor groups who recognize that children are our future, and investing in them is the best path toward realizing a better, brighter future.

English Tutorials

In collaboration with bridging future and satyalok, the English learning sessions are being conducted for students in nearby villages.

Satyendra Narayan Rai is at the forefront as their teacher and mentor. The current model is being practiced every Saturday for 1 hour via zoom, where Catherine meets with a small group of 4-6 students in grades 9 and 10.

These sessions consist of English comprehension where Catherine assigns passages from articles, clips from famous television shows as well as American and English literature, while the children attempt at translating these clips and passages prior to class and practice reading out loud with each other.

The purpose of these classes is to get the students comfortable using the language, using everyday language, and expanding their vocabulary to include both elementary and advanced language.


For anyone interested in volunteering to read aloud with the children, please contact us for more information.

Physical activity

After the lesson completes, the children spend time doing physical activities. This includes dancing to their favorite music, playing Kabaddi, and more.

Fun Fact - Kabaddi is a game played between two teams of seven players, in which individuals take turns to chase and try to touch members of the opposing team without being captured by them.
